The Fuel Bag

Can you feel
the food?

Transforming the secondary school lunchtime experience into a high-concentration, community-building activity package in direct alignment with existing food services.

Vision & Aspirations

Healthy Connections

Lunches should spark youthfulness, problem solving, and higher concentration parameters across classrooms.

In-House Synergy

Partnering directly with internal chefs at Aspens to inject vibrant flavors directly into school meal deals.

Primary Goal: Drive engagement using autonomy, puzzle mechanics, and gamification.

Fuel Bag Specifications

  • Mains: Sandwiches (Cheese, Tuna & Cucumber) & Wraps (Chicken & Halloumi)
  • Snacks: Oatcakes, pita chips, red pepper hummus, bean dip, or cookies
  • Drinks: Varied flavored waters & standard Radnor Fizz matching Aspens stock
  • Riddle Card: Low-stakes riddles and map-based orienteering challenges
  • Rewards: Token mechanics redeemable at the school Golden Ticket Shop
  • The Bag: Formed out of 100% recycled paper ensuring structural school sustainability
